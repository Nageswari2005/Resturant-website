About Us

Founded in 2005, Amma’s Flame celebrates India's rich culinary traditions with refined, heartfelt recipes passed down through generations.

We combine warm hospitality, premium ingredients, and timeless ambiance to give our guests an unforgettable dining experience.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Nivedita is the creative force behind Amma’s Flame. Born and raised in a traditional South Indian household, she learned the secrets of spices and slow cooking from her grandmother — the original "Amma" behind the name.

With over 20 years of culinary experience and a deep love for regional Indian cuisine, Chef Nivedita has crafted a menu that balances authenticity with a modern twist. Her dishes are a tribute to tradition, using family recipes, seasonal ingredients, and a passion that shines through in every bite.

Under her leadership, Amma’s Flame has grown from a humble eatery to a destination for food lovers seeking genuine South Indian flavors in a fine dining setting.
